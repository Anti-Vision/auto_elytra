## How It Works
- **Automatic Swapping:** Double-jump and it just equips your Elytra, so you can fly off into the sunset. 
- **Auto Return:** Touch the ground and your chestplate’s back on.
- **Toggle On/Off:** You can toggle it on and off only by pressing a key (You can change it in the keybind menu).
